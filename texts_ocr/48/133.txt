As for Reliability and completeness, the Report is supported by a structured information system, processing finan-
cial and non-financial information while ensuring their homogeneity and reliability. The performance indicators are
used in the business management in line with the strategic plan. They refer to the whole Group unless otherwise
indicated.

In accordance with the Consistency and Comparability principle, the report includes information that is consistent with

the previous year, unless otherwise indicated, and the strategic objectives announced to the market.
