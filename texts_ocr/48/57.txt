Revolving credit facilities

Assicurazioni Generali has revolving credit facilities for a
total amount of € 4 billion. They represent, in line with best
market practice, an efficient tool to protect the Group's fi-
nancial flexibility in case of adverse scenarios.

The two facilities syndicated with a value of € 2 billion
each, have a duration of 3 and 5 years.
