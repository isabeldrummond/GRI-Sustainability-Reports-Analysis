Pierluigi and Giancarlo de’ Stefani found SIT La Precisa, a precision mechanics company,
in Padua, Italy

Continuous growth leads to the opening of the first foreign branch in the Netherlands
Other European branches soon follow in the UK, France, Turkey, Poland, Germany, and

the Czech Republic

SIT goes global with direct operations, starting in Australia with a branch in Melbourne

The expansion continues with the opening of a branch in the United States SIT sells its
first electronic board for gas appliances

Direct operations begin in China with the opening of a branch in Shanghai
