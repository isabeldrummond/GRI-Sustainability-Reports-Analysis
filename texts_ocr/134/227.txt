The Group is a lessor of right-of-use assets which it accounts for as operating leases.

It is not required to reclassify those leases in which it is the lessor under IFRS 16, except for subleases. The
Group has assessed its classification of subleases considering the right-of-use asset rather than the underlying

asset and has found that they comply with the definition of operating leases as per IFRS 16.
