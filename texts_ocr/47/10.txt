The activities of GEDI Gruppo Editoriale
Group Profile

GEDI

GRUPPO EDITORIALE

GEDI is one of the leading publishing groups in Italy. It operates in all sectors of communication,
from the daily and periodical press and publishing sectors to radio, internet and advertising sales.

On 27 June 2017, the merger was completed into GEDI of the ITEDI Group, publisher of the
newspapers La Stampa and il Secolo XIX. As a result of this transaction, GEDI acquired control of
the ITEDI Group, which entered into the scope of consolidation on 30 June 2017.
