The industrial sector of laser systems for industrial
manufacturing:

The El.En. Group creates for the industrial sector laser
systems for cutting and marking; these systems can be
adapted for multiple uses and can be used on many
different kinds of materials. They also produce laser
sources with performance that can be incremented in
terms of the speed and quality of the processes, reliability,

safety and electrical efficiency.

The Group also operates in the sector of restoration of
works of art: for many years they have contributed to
the conservation of the artistic and historical heritage all
over the world by proposing state-of-the-art solutions
that are minimally invasive, developed in cooperation
with the most important research centers and tested in
important restoration laboratories.
