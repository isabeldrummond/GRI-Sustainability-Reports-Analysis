Selection, talent attraction

and retention

One of Cerved's strategic assets are its people who, with their
strong professional background and personal attitude, generate
value for the Group and are the main driver of its success

and growth. For this reason, it is fundamental to enhance the
selection process, training and the development of skills, which
are key to strengthening business competitiveness and growth.

SELECTION AND ONBOARDING
PROCESS

Empathy and resilience are the key
elements of the mix of skills held by
the approximately 2,600 people who
work at Cerved in Italy, Romania,
Greece and Switzerland. The Group
invests in the selection and devel-
opment of its talents and enhances
them so that they can perform to
their full potential, in a cohesive and
innovative environment.
