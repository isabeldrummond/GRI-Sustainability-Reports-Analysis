Conclusions
Based on the work performed, nothing has come to our attention that causes us to believe that the NFS
of Alperia Group as of 31 December 2019 has not been prepared, in all material respects, in compliance

with articles 3 and 4 of the Decree, with the GRI Standards and with the “G4 Sector Disclosure —
Electric Utilities”.
