ABOUT THIS REPORT

This Sustainability Report represents a big step for
Italpreziosi, which has always silently operated in
the most sustainable way by supporting its
stakeholders. With this Report, we try to further
promote the concept of sustainability in the
precious metal chain.

Much of the information in this Sustainability Report
has been verified through internal and third-party
audits.

The Report will be drawn up annually.
