In line with the contents of the Code of Ethics on the management of human capital, the Isagro Group undertakes to
make sure that the work environment favours equal opportunities and develops a culture based on the valorization
of diversity. In confirmation of this commitment, at 31 December 2016, there were 123 women working for the
company, that is, 20% of the entire staff, 7 of whom belong to the “Directors/executives” category.
