ICAM and its support
to the UN’s Sustainable
Development Goals

In 2015, the United Nations launched their 2030 Agenda

for Sustainable Development, a global action plan aimed
at ending global issues such as poverty, hunger, lack of
accessible education, climate change, gender inequality,

lack of. access to clean water and energy.

The Agenda outlines the 17 Sustainable Development
Goals (otherwise known as SDGs) and their 169 targets
that need to be met by 2030. ICAM supports and
promotes the UN’ initiative, with the awareness that its
approach and business vision are coherent and aligned
with the stated purpose of the action plan.

ICAM has decided to actively support the initiative and
contribute, through its activity and production of ethical
and sustainable chocolate, to the 2030 Agenda and to the
achievement of SDGs
