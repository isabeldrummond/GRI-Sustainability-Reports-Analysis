traditional system, which includes the presence of. a Board
of Directors with administrative roles, and a Board of
Auditors tasked with supervising the administration, both
nominated by an assembly. For the structure of the Board
of Directors, the company uses the CONSOB model,

including the presence of. two independent advisors:

In order to ensure the full respect of lawfulness, integrity
and transparency principles, in 2012 ICAM adopted an
Organizational, Management, and Control Model
(Model 231, in accordance with the Italian law
2311/2001). In doing so, ICAM implemented suitable
organizational procedures aimed at preventing preventing
corporate crimes and promoting, among the actors
operating on behalf of the Company (both internally and
externally), the respect for the rule of law.
