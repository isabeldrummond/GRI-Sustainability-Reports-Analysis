¢ PRODUCTS FOR INDUSTRY:

© PRIVATE LABEL:

an extensive experience in production and technology allows

consists in producing multiple varieties of bars and pralines,

ducts are created with conventional, organic, and fair-trade
raw materials. The Company is able to supply a completely
customisable solution, ranging from the development of new
recipes to the elaborate study of the packaging, specific to to
the target market and the sales channels of each commercial
partner.

° The Vanini brand: Vaninis premium chocolate line is
