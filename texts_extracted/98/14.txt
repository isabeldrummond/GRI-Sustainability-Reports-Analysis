Code of Ethics: the principles of Orsero’s daily actions 
 
Orsero S.p.A acts in compliance with the principles of loyalty, correctness, transparency, 
responsibility, freedom, dignity of human beings and respect for diversity and rejects any 
discrimination based on sex, race, language, personal and social conditions and religious 
and political beliefs. 
 
 
 
 
The Code of Ethics is the document that defines the set of ethical and social rules to 
which corporate representatives must adhere; it can be defined as the "Constitutional 
Charter" of a company, as it establishes moral rights and duties, defining the ethical-so-
cial responsibility of each participant in the Company’s organization.
