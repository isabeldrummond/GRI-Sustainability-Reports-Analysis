Contract management 
The guidelines with regard to procurement, in accordance with the Group(cid:859)s Code of Ethics and the 
organisational model pursuant to Italian Legislative Decree No. 231/2001 and the related (cid:862)procurement(cid:863) 
protocol  determine  the  underlying  principles  of  the  Group(cid:859)s  procurement  activities  in  the  event  of 
acquisition of goods, services and work necessary for the performance of activities carried out under free 
market conditions and subject to public works contracts (Code of the public contracts Italian Legislative 
Decree No. 50/2016, as amended by the corrective decree No. 56/2017).  
The use of sub-contracts

with full compliance of AcegasApsAmga as well.
