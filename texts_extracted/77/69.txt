The Non-Financial Report
The Isagro 2016 Non-Financial Report is written in compliance with the Sustainability Reporting Standards 
established in 2016 by the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI), on the basis of the “Core” option, which requires the 
reporting of at least one indicator for each of the issues that emerged as material from the Materiality analysis.
